WHO WE ARE

HorsePower for Kids is an outdoor animal sanctuary and family fun farm located in Northwest Tampa near Oldsmar and Westchase. We offer...

  • Petting zoo
  • Pony rides
  • Playground and picnic area
  • Child-size zip line, bench swings
  • Train that rides around the grounds to see all the animals!
